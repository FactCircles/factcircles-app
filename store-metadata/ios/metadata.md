## App Store Description (4000 chars max)
FactCircles is a confidential, AI-powered conflict resolution platform with a 97% success rate — helping families, coworkers, and communities resolve disputes in 55 minutes or less, for less than alternative processes.

WHAT IS A FACTCIRCLE?
FactCircles is based on the restorative justice principles from "Live Connected" by John Richards. Instead of adversarial processes that create winners and losers, FactCircles creates a safe, structured space where everyone is heard — and a genuine solution is found.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Start a session and invite participants by name or phone contact
2. Grok AI guides everyone through three powerful questions:
   • "What happened?"
   • "Who is affected?"
   • "How can this be resolved?"
3. Each participant takes turns responding — a rotating icon shows whose turn it is
4. Grok AI empathetically summarizes all responses and recommends a solution
5. Save your session record for future reference

KEY FEATURES
• 97% success rate in conflict resolution
• Completely anonymous outside of your private session
• Grok AI facilitates with empathy — no judgment, no shame
• 55-minute structured sessions with optional extension
• Story Balloon detection — Grok identifies and gently challenges assumption-based accusations
• Distress monitoring — if a participant appears in crisis, Grok responds immediately
• Milestone badges for completed sessions
• Save session summaries and solutions
• Contact import for easy participant invitations
• Apple Pay support
• Secure, encrypted sessions

WHO IS IT FOR?
• Divorcing or co-parenting couples
• Families navigating conflict
• Workplace disputes
• Neighbor disagreements
• Friend groups working through tension
• Anyone seeking resolution without expensive legal processes

PRICING
$30 per participant per session. You choose who pays — cover everyone, or split equally. Session records can be saved for $30 per participant at the end.

PRIVACY
Participants are completely anonymous outside of their session. We never sell your data. Sessions are encrypted and private.
